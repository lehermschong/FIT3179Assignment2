Most Popular Vaccine Manufacturers
Due to extensive research by multiple different countries and organizations, various vaccines have been developed to combat the viral outbreak. mRNA vaccines such as Pfizer/BioNTech and Moderna have been statiscally proven to be the most effective vaccines. This makes them the most highly sought and produced vaccines all around the world.

Vaccination Trends per Region
Vaccination efforts around the world have been unrelenting with hopes of defeating the pandemic. Asia is currently the continent with the most vaccinated people, and Europe is the continent with the highest percentage of vaccinated population. Africa is poorest continents and is unable to afford vaccinations, hence the low numbers.
Due to the recent surge of the Delta-variant cases in Asia around August, vaccination efforts have skyrocketed since then to prepare nations for another outbreak.

Fully Vaccinated Age Groups
Many countries have different vaccination strategies. However, one common part shared is that more than 50% of the doses are given to citizens aged 50 and above. This is because they have weaker immunity to the virus and have a higher mortality rate. Vaccines are given to them to prevent excessive deaths.

Conclusion
Vaccines are a very important factor in successfully combating the COVID-19 pandemic. In nearly all countries in the world, vaccination efforts are still ongoing and will not be seeing much of a stop until most of the nation is fully vaccinated against the virus. That said, efforts and countermeasures need to be taken more serious and effectively in order to reduce the severity of the virus to just another common flu.
